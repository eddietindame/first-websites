Interests.

Guitar

I started playing the electric guitar around 9 years ago. I decided to start playing after seeing an episode of wife swap where a kid was learning to play and I thought it looked easy. I was wrong. Much practice got me some pretty decent skills for my age (at the time) but I got lazy and now I'm just average (in my opinion). I also dabble in bass and acoustic from time to time. I play mostly metal and punk stuff but lately I've been working on my finger picking and playing more classical tunes. I play the LTD MH-400 (pictured on the right).

Videogames

Videogames are a pretty big part of my life. My first games console was a Nintendo 64. I've been a fan ever since. My current faveorite game series are The Legend of Zelda, Pokémon and Street Fighter. I've been enjoy the competetive scene in Pokémon and Street Fighter a lot lately. As there aren't that many people around me to play with (apart from online), I spend a lot of time watching competetive matches and tournaments on Youtube.

Anime

While not as die-hard as some, I do enjoy a good anime binge from time to time. There's nothing like getting lost in a new series on netflix. I've liked anime since I was young, starting with watching whatever was on Citv (Pokemon, Digimon, Cardcaptors, Flint the Time Detective etc). My faveorite anime that I recently watched is Fullmetal Alchemist. It had such great character development and pacing that I've never been attatched to a group of characters more, whether anime or otherwise. I highly recemond it to any fan of Japanese cartoons.
